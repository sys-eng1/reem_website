Mock Thesis Defense Practice

Welcome, and thank you for expressing interest in our Mock Thesis Defense Practice! Our goal is to help you confidently present your research and excel during your thesis defense. This comprehensive package is designed to provide you with the tools and experience you need to shine on your big day. Here's what you'll receive:

  • Video Lecture: A detailed guide on mastering your thesis presentation, including strategies for structuring your talk and engaging your audience.
  • Slide Review and Editing: We'll carefully review your presentation slides, suggest improvements, and help you enhance the visual and data presentation aspects.
  • One-on-One Consultation: A 1.5-hour personalized session to discuss your specific needs, address any fears, and focus on areas where you need the most help.
  • Mock Thesis Defense: A 2-hour simulation of your actual thesis defense day, including a timed presentation followed by a Q&A session, just like the real thing. Afterward, we'll provide constructive feedback to help you refine your approach.

As a special offer, if you register before the end of September, you'll receive a $100 discount on the entire package, making it just $500 instead of the regular $600. We're excited to work with you and help you achieve success in your thesis defense. Let's make your presentation the best it can be!
